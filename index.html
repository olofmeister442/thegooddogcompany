Our Services

Daycare

Our daycare service provides a safe, fun, and stimulating environment for your dog to socialize and play throughout the day. We offer:

  • Supervised group play sessions
  • Regular potty breaks
  • Fresh water and treats
  • Rest periods in comfortable areas
  • Daily report cards

Enrichment Activities

Puzzle Games

Mental stimulation through interactive toys and puzzles

Agility Training

Basic obstacle courses for physical exercise

Sensory Play

Engaging activities that stimulate all senses

Boarding

When you need to be away, your dog will feel right at home in our comfortable boarding facility. We provide:

  • Private sleeping areas
  • 24/7 supervision
  • Regular exercise and playtime
  • Meal and medication management
  • Daily updates and photos

Training

Our professional training programs help your dog learn and grow. We offer:

  • Basic obedience training
  • Behavior modification
  • Puppy socialization
  • Advanced training
  • Private and group sessions
