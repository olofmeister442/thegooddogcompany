Born from Passion. Built for Dogs.

The Good Dog Company is founded by Monisha Bhatia, who brings together the best of passion, perfection and purpose in all that we do.

About Monisha:

"The realisation happened when, as a dog trainer, I kept seeing the same pattern - dogs weren't misbehaving as much as they lacked the right outlets for their energy. The gap became clearer when I noticed that even after training, many dogs still struggled with boredom and unwanted behaviours because their natural instincts had nowhere to go."

"I wanted to create a space where dogs could truly thrive—a place where they could spend a few hours or a whole day engaging in activities that enrich their lives. By tapping into their natural instincts, they become happier, more balanced, and fulfilled in the long run."

- Monisha

Fun Fact: The splash pool was inspired by her own dog's love for rain showers.

Monisha holds a degree in Assistance Dog Education from Bergin University, an MA in Human-Animal Interactions from the University of Stirling, and is an LSR alumna. With 6+ years of experience (and her own dog Azy!), she blends expertise and heart to give your pup the best care possible.
